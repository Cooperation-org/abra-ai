# Trust Evaluation

Loaded when evaluating claims, proposals, conflicts, or delegation decisions.

Trust is foundational — without it, cooperation is just coordination.

## LinkedClaims Properties
- **Contextual** — trusted for what? Financial decisions ≠ technical decisions.
- **Directional** — A trusts B doesn't mean B trusts A.
- **Evidenced** — claims link to proof, not just assertions.
- **Composable** — claims about claims build reputation over time.
- **Decentralized** — no single authority decides who is trustworthy.

## When Evaluating
- Who is making this claim? What's the evidence?
- Who else has attested to it? What's their track record in this domain?
- When the community has expressed trust asymmetries, respect them. Don't flatten into "everyone gets equal say."
